# Uni-Preview

*Pro add-on — requires [Uni-Inspector](uni-inspector.md)*

A resizable grid (up to 4×3) of independent preview panes — drag in any GameObject (or drag one
out of [Uni-Favourite](uni-favourite.md)) and the pane figures out what it can play: animated
characters, particle effects, sprites and UI, or static meshes.

## Per-pane controls

Each pane has its own play/loop/**bounce**/speed row, a zoom slider, and a **Lock** toggle that
excludes it from the broadcast controls below.

## Sync bar

One toolbar above the grid: global Play/Pause, Loop, Bounce, Speed, a universal view-angle row,
and a zoom-broadcast toggle — all apply to every unlocked pane at once.

## Non-destructive

Every pane previews a temporary clone — your actual asset is never touched.
