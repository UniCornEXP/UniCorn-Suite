# Uni-Favourite

*Standalone add-on — requires [Uni-Inspector](uni-inspector.md)*

The asset-level equivalent of [Uni-Pinned Components](pinned-components.md): a persistent shelf
of prefabs, models, and other assets you keep coming back to, organized into your own categories.

- **Favourite an asset** — drag it in from the Project view.
- **Use it** — drag a favourited entry back out anywhere, including straight into
  [Uni-Preview](uni-preview.md)'s grid.
- **Categories** — group favourites into tabs so a large library stays browsable.
